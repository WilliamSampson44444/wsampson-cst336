C++ first appeared in 1985, originally referred to as "C with Classes" by its creator Bjarne Stroustrup, of Denmark.
Since then, it has grown to be one of the most popular programming languages, thanks to an emphasis on performance and efficiency inherited from C, combined with aspects of object-oriented programming.
